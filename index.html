Frequently Asked Questions

What does an SEO specialist in Sri Lanka do?
An SEO specialist improves your website's visibility on Google by optimizing technical setup, content, and authority signals, ensuring your business reaches more potential customers.
How much does SEO cost in Sri Lanka?
SEO pricing varies depending on project scope. On average, businesses invest between LKR 75,000 – 200,000 per month for professional SEO services.
How long does it take to rank a website?
Depending on competition and site health, visible results typically take 3–6 months.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes. Dinusha has managed SEO campaigns for businesses in Australia, the UK, and the USA in addition to Sri Lanka.
