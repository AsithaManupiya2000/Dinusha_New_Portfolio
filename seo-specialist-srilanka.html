SEO Specialist Sri Lanka - Frequently Asked Questions

What does an SEO specialist in Sri Lanka do?

An SEO specialist in Sri Lanka optimizes websites to improve their visibility on search engines like Google. This includes technical optimizations (site speed, mobile-friendliness), content optimization (keyword research, on-page SEO), and authority building (link building, digital PR) specifically tailored for both local Sri Lankan markets and international audiences.

As an experienced SEO specialist, I help Sri Lankan businesses rank higher in search results, drive qualified traffic to their websites, and ultimately increase conversions and revenue.

How much does SEO cost in Sri Lanka?

SEO pricing in Sri Lanka varies based on the scope of work, competition level, and business goals. Generally, professional SEO services range from:

  • Basic SEO packages: LKR 75,000 - 125,000 per month
  • Comprehensive SEO campaigns: LKR 125,000 - 200,000 per month
  • Enterprise-level SEO: LKR 200,000+ per month

I offer customized pricing based on your specific needs and provide transparent cost breakdowns with clear expectations of deliverables and outcomes.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically follow this timeline:

  • 1-3 months: Technical improvements, initial indexing changes, baseline establishment
  • 3-6 months: Noticeable ranking improvements for less competitive keywords, traffic increases
  • 6-12 months: Significant ranking improvements for competitive keywords, substantial traffic growth
  • 12+ months: Authority establishment, sustainable traffic, and conversion growth

These timelines can vary based on your website's current state, competition level, and the consistency of SEO implementation.

What's the difference between an SEO specialist, expert, and consultant in Sri Lanka?

While these terms are often used interchangeably, there are subtle differences:

  • SEO Specialist: Focuses on hands-on implementation of SEO strategies (technical, on-page, off-page)
  • SEO Expert: Has deep knowledge and proven success across multiple SEO disciplines and industries
  • SEO Consultant: Provides strategic guidance, audits, and recommendations but may not handle implementation

As your SEO specialist in Sri Lanka, I offer the complete package: strategic consulting combined with hands-on implementation to ensure your SEO success.
